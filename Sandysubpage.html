SANDY

Born: unknown (circa 1996)

Adopted: 03/24/2008

Died: 09/27/2012
The revolving door of senior dogs continued when we adopted 12-year old Sandy who was a beautiful, gentle and calm redhead. Although the adoption papers describe Sandy as a Dachshund mix, we think she was part Corgi as she had a very thick and luxurious coat and a Corgi-like body with short legs. She was euthanized at age 16 after a very long life. Ira keeps Sandy's ashes in a cabinet near his dresser.
