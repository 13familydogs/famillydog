MAX

Born: unknown (senior)

Adopted: 03/2006

Died: 08/2006
It seemed cruel to allow an old dog to remain locked in a kennel at the end of his life so we adopted Max, an Australian Cattle Dog, who not only was a senior, but also had blood blisters on most of his body. Max was an excellent herder -- herding all of us as he felt appropriate. He was also a very efficient business man -- doing his "business" quickly and routinely. He lived with us for only 5-months before being euthanized, but we made sure that the time he did have with us was well lived.
